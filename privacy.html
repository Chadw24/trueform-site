Privacy Policy

Effective:

Trueform (“we”, “us”) is a personal finance app. We collect only what we need to operate the service and never sell personal data.

Data we process

  • Account info you provide (name, email).

How we use data

  • Provide and secure the service.
  • Notify you about important changes.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent abuse.

Third parties

We use trusted vendors such as Stripe (payments) and analytics providers. These processors handle data on our behalf under agreements consistent with this policy.

Your choices

  • Access/update your info by contacting support.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of non‑essential emails at any time.

Data retention & security

We retain account data while your subscription is active and delete it within a reasonable period after closure unless required for legal or tax reasons. We apply industry‑standard security measures and never store full card details.
